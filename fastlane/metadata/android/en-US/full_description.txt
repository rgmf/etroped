Etroped is a GPS-tracking app for tracking your bike, running and trekking activities.

Features:
* Display live tracking data: speed, average speed, altitude information from GPS, time, pace...
* Display last 5 weeks stats.
* Activities details with stats.
* General stats: summary, monthly and yearly.
* OpenStreetMap track visualization.
* Charts representing stats and data: altitude, speed, pace...
* Experimental Pebble integration (only tested on Pebble 2).
